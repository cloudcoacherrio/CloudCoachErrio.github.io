My Professional Journey

Where I've been. Where I am. Where I'm headed.

The Foundation Years With over a decade in the technology landscape, I've been fortunate to experience the evolution of IT from multiple perspectives. My journey began in hardware and software support, where I developed a foundation for understanding technical challenges from the ground up.

The Growth Phase As technology evolved, so did my path. I've had the privilege of wearing many hats—from database administration and network infrastructure to CRM development and system administration. Each role has added a unique dimension to my understanding of how technology ecosystems function and interconnect.

The Cloud Transition In recent years, my focus has shifted toward cloud computing, where I've explored migration strategies, security frameworks, networking architectures, and application development in cloud environments. Working with technologies like AWS Elastic Kubernetes Service and Python has particularly sparked my passion for building scalable, resilient systems.

Present Role Currently, I serve as a Technical Trainer at the AWS Cloud Institute, where I have the rewarding opportunity to help others navigate their cloud journey. There's something special about witnessing someone's "lightbulb moment" when a complex concept suddenly clicks.

Future Direction As I look forward, I'm actively developing my expertise in AI Engineering—a field that fascinates me with its potential to transform how we build and interact with technology. The intersection of cloud computing and artificial intelligence presents challenges that energize me daily.

Knowledge Sharing For those who prefer learning through demonstration, I regularly share technical video content across my social platforms. Feel free to connect with me @CloudCoachErrio to explore cloud concepts together.

Personal Philosophy Every day brings new technologies to master and innovative problems to solve—and I wouldn't have it any other way.
